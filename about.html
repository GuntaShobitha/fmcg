2012 – Today

From five farms to a movement

What began as a weekend project between five family farms in the northern valley has grown into a community of 220+ organic growers, food scientists, delivery riders and — most importantly — 48,000+ households who chose to eat honestly.

We haven't changed our promise since day one: nothing artificial, nothing hidden, nothing hurried.

The Journey

Farm to your kitchen in under 18 hours

We harvest at dawn, chill within the hour, and pack in compostable materials at our regional micro-hubs. Your box is on a bike by mid-morning and at your door by evening.

  • 🌅 04:30 — Harvest starts on partner farms
  • 🚛 07:00 — Cold transport to micro-hubs
  • 📦 10:00 — Hand-packed with your name on the box
  • 🏡 16:00 — At your doorstep, still crisp

2022
